Unit Converter

Unit Converter is a simple, user-friendly web application that lets users quickly convert between common metric and imperial units. It focuses on three categories—length (meters ⇄ feet), volume (liters ⇄ gallons), and mass (kilograms ⇄ pounds). Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it features a clean, responsive interface and real-time results, making it ideal for students, professionals, and anyone who needs quick unit conversions.

Password Generator

The Password Generator is a simple and user-friendly web application that helps users create strong, random, and secure passwords instantly. It allows users to choose the password length and include numbers or symbols for extra complexity. With just one click, it generates two random passwords that can easily be copied and used. The app also includes a light and dark mode for a comfortable user experience, making it a convenient and reliable tool for anyone who wants to protect their online accounts.
